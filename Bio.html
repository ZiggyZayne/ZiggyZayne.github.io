Zayne Lockhart, Critter Photographer:

Hello! My name is Zayne Lockhart and I'm an amateur photographer based out of Florence, Alabama. I'm particularly fond of shooting birds, but I also love getting shots of dogs/pets. You'll see a lot of my girlfriend's and my dogs, Peach and Siri!

I got my first camera in April of 2024, and it immediately inspired me to get out, connect with nature, and create art that I'm truly proud of. I gravitated to bird photography very quickly. The challenge and excitement of getting a good, sharp bird in flight shot makes me absolutely ecstatic. I hope that in visiting my website and looking through my work, some of that excitement spills over into your world and puts a smile on your face!

If you want to know more, please reach out via my Contact page! Thanks for stopping by!
