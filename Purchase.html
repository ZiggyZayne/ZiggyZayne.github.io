If you are interested in purchasing a print, here's a quick guide!

First: Thank you so much for choosing to support my art!

As far as shipping prints goes, it's a bit of a complicated subject at this stage. I can't justify the price of a site like PicFair that handles printing and shipping. So I have to pay the printing costs and shipping costs myself for each individual order. Therefore, it's tricky to price. So If you want a print, please email me a screenshot of the images you would like a print of, your shipping info, and the size you would like. I will then get the necessary quotes and estimate shipping and provide you with the price, with a little head-room for profit. Then, after payment is sent via Paypal or Venmo (links in my Contact page!) I will send your prints!

You can also choose to just leave a small donation via either of those methods, if getting a print isn't your thing. Or even just shoot me an email telling me which photo is your favorite!! I'm just thankful you've taken the time to look at the art I've made, and I hope it has brought you some semblance of happiness!
